Mobileshop Management System

Our Mobile Shop Management System is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline the operations of mobile shops. Developed using Java NetBeans and integrated with SQL and XAMPP, this system empowers administrators to efficiently manage their inventory, sales, and financial records.

  • Inventory Management

    • Admins can easily add, update, and manage product details, including stock levels, prices, and descriptions.

  • Sales and Billing

    • The system facilitates smooth transactions by generating accurate bills for purchases, ensuring seamless customer experiences.

  • Reporting and Analytics

    • Gain insights into business performance with comprehensive reports on product lists, daily sales, and profitability. Analyze data to make informed decisions and optimize operations.

  • Profit Calculation

    • By tracking expenses and revenue, the system enables administrators to calculate profits and monitor financial health effectively.
